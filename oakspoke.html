Oakspoke is an experiment.

                                    

                                        
The Oakspoke Hypothesis:

                                        
"Given the right social conditions, Participant A will accept acoorn from Participant B in exchange for a beer."

                                    

                                    
                                    
How It Works

                                    
Claim a spot on the map

                                    Pick a location. That's your home on the Oakspoke network.

                                    
                                    
Send and receive acoorns

                                    Exchange acoorns with people at other locations.

                                    
                                    
Run a forager

                                    A forager is a small, independent robot that checks the ledger to ensure everything is accurate and consistent. Run one, and you earn acoorns for helping keep the system honest.

                                    
                                    
What Oakspoke Is (and Isn't)

                                    
    
                                        
  • Not a get-rich-quick crypto scheme
    • 
                                        
  • Not backed by promises or hype
    • 
                                        
  • Just a simple system designed to see what happens when people decide something has value
